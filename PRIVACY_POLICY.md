# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 22, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Glowy** ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Information Automatically Collected
When you use the App, we automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with the App through third-party services:
*   **Usage Data:** We use **Firebase Analytics** to collect information on how the App is accessed and used (e.g., screen views, button clicks).
*   **Crash Reports:** We use **Firebase Crashlytics** to collect technical logs when the App crashes to help us fix bugs.
*   **Device Identifiers:** We use **Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)** which may collect your device's advertising ID (like IDFA on iOS or AAID on Android) to show personalized ads.
*   **Subscription Data:** We use **RevenueCat** to manage and track your subscription status. This does not include your credit card information, which is handled exclusively by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

### b. Information You Provide
The App is designed to be used without requiring a user account. We do not currently collect or store your name, email address, or other direct identifiers on our own servers.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data to:
*   Provide and maintain the App's functionality.
*   Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve user experience.
*   Identify, diagnose, and fix technical issues and crashes.
*   Process and manage subscriptions via third-party providers.
*   Display relevant advertisements.

## 3. Sharing Your Information
We share information with the following third-party service providers to help us operate the App:
*   **Google (Firebase & AdMob):** For analytics, crash reporting, and advertising.
*   **RevenueCat:** For subscription management.
*   **Apple App Store / Google Play Store:** For payment processing and App distribution.

We may also disclose your information to comply with applicable laws and regulations or to respond to a lawful request for information.

## 4. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data, such as the right to access, correct, or delete the information we have about you. Since we do not store personal identifiers directly, most data is stored with the third-party providers mentioned above.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
